Chromosome X

For this project, I conducted extensive research about the role of the X chromosome. I wanted to use data about the genes on this chromosome and the proteins they encode to tell an interesting, easy-to-understand story. The interactive begins with a few informative pages detailing the foundational knowledge of genetics. The final chromosome section involves my illustration of a single X chromosome. Hovering over the bands of the image reveals information about specific genes and additional facts. While only a few genes are included out of the hundreds possible, this project conveys the diverse range of functions the X chromosome's DNA fulfills.

This project was created entirely in Processing with Java

Woven Through Time

Woven Through Time is an interactive touchscreen museum display created for the Ospedale Degli Innocenti in Florence, Italy. The project guides museum visitors through some of the paintings displayed in the museum that feature historical textiles, and the goal of the exhibit is to highlight the relevance of textiles and the Silk Guilds of Renaissance Florence to the Ospedale orphanage.

This project was researched and created in Adobe InDesign by Julia Ehlert and Samantha Steadman for Studio Arts College International (SACI) in Florence, Italy.

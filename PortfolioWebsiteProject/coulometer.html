How a Coulometer Works

How a Coulometer Works is an animated motion graphics video that I produced for UIC Inc as their Media and Marketing Intern. I researched the function of the carbon detecting coulometric systems, scripted this process video, created assets in Illustrator, and animated in AfterEffects.

To learn more about coulometry, check out this explanatory presentation I also made for UIC: What Is Coulometry?
